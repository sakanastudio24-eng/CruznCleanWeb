### FAQ Questions + Answers
- How many vehicles can I submit in one day?
- The booking flow supports up to 4 vehicles per customer per day, and actual capacity depends on the length of the selected services.
- What are your booking hours?
- Standard booking hours are Monday through Saturday from 8am to 6pm.
- How long do the main services take?
- Maintenance Detail is roughly 90 minutes. Full Interior or Full Exterior is about 3 hours. A Full Reset usually lands around 6 to 8 hours depending on condition.
- Can I book coatings or paint correction without a detail package?
- Yes. Protection and correction services can be booked on their own, though final prep requirements are confirmed after inspection.
- What service areas can book online?
- Online booking is focused on these service-area cities: Yorba Linda, Placentia, Fullerton, Anaheim, Brea, Orange, La Habra, Buena Park, Cypress, Irvine, Huntington Beach, Costa Mesa, Tustin, Garden Grove, Santa Ana. Standard online ZIP coverage includes 92801-92809, 92821, 92823, 92831-92835, 92840-92846, 92865-92871, 92886-92887, 92602-92620, 92626-92628, 92683-92685. If your ZIP is outside that area, request a quote so travel and availability can be reviewed.
- What if my ZIP code is outside the service area?
- Use Request a Quote instead of standard booking. Outside-area appointments may still be possible, but they need a manual review for travel time, scheduling, and final approval.
- How does pricing change by vehicle size?
- Listed pricing starts with sedans and coupes. Small SUVs and trucks add 20%, large SUVs and trucks add 40%, and vans or very lifted vehicles add 50%.
- How is final pricing confirmed?
- Final pricing is confirmed after inspection and may increase for larger vehicles, excess dirt, pet hair, staining, ride height, or condition-related labor.
- Do you need water or power on-site?
- You do not need to provide water. Share access, parking, gate, or setup notes during booking so the appointment can be approved correctly.
- How should I prepare my vehicle before appointment time?
- Remove loose items and personal belongings before arrival. Full compartments will not be cleaned, and the vehicle should be parked in an accessible location with at least 8 feet of clearance from other vehicles.
- What should I do while the service is being completed?
- Please stay clear of the vehicle during active service to help prevent accidents or injury. Be ready for key handoff at the scheduled time and available for any needed approval.
- Can my quote change after booking?
- Yes. The service quote is subject to change if the vehicle was booked incorrectly, condition is different than described, or the job needs extended attention after inspection.
- Does my deposit need to be confirmed?
- Yes. Deposit confirmation is required before the appointment is treated as locked in.
- What should I know about coatings and correction results?
- Paint correction improves defects but may not remove every scratch or imperfection. Ceramic coating longevity depends on aftercare and environmental exposure.
- How often should I book a maintenance detail?
- Most maintained vehicles benefit from recurring service every 4 to 8 weeks, depending on driving conditions and storage habits.
- What payment methods do you accept?
- Cash, Zelle, Venmo, Cash App, and PayPal are accepted. Debit and credit support may be added later through the booking site.

## Terms of Service

### Intro
- Terms of Service
- These terms outline appointment approval, pricing, deposits, weather rescheduling, and service limits for Cruizn Clean mobile detailing.

### 1. Appointments & Scheduling
- All appointments must be booked in advance.
- Same-day booking requests may require rush fees.
- A confirmation is sent after the booking is approved.
- Please be on time or prepared for arrival to avoid delays or rescheduling.

### 2. Deposits
- A non-refundable deposit of $25-$100 may be required to secure your appointment.
- Any required deposit is applied toward the total service cost.
- No deposit means the booking is not guaranteed.

### 3. Cancellations & Rescheduling
- At least 24 hours notice is required to cancel or reschedule.
- Deposits may be forfeited for late cancellations or no-shows.
- Emergencies are handled case-by-case.

### 4. Pricing
- Listed prices are starting prices for sedans and coupes.
- Small SUVs and trucks add 20%, large SUVs and trucks add 40%, and vans or very lifted vehicles add 50%.
- Pricing can increase based on vehicle size, ride height, excess dirt, grime, stains, pet hair, or condition.
- Final price is confirmed after inspection.

### 5. Vehicle Condition
- Remove personal belongings before the appointment. Full compartments will not be cleaned.
- Cruizn Clean is not responsible for lost or damaged personal items left in the vehicle.

### 6. Service Limitations
- Best-effort results are provided, but not all stains, scratches, or imperfections can be fully removed.
- Paint correction and polishing improve defects but may not eliminate them completely.

### 7. Ceramic Coating / Protection Services
- Proper aftercare is required to maintain durability.
- Longevity claims such as 3-year or 6-year coatings depend on maintenance and environmental conditions.
- There are no guarantees against improper care after service completion.

### 8. Weather Policy (Mobile Services)
- Appointments may be rescheduled due to rain, extreme heat, or unsafe conditions.
- Cruizn Clean will contact you as early as possible if weather requires a change.

### 9. Payment
- Payment is due upon completion unless otherwise agreed.
- Accepted forms: Cash, Zelle, Venmo, Cash App, and PayPal.
- Debit and credit support may be added later through the booking site.
- Checks are not accepted.

### 10. Satisfaction Guarantee
- If you are not satisfied, notify Cruizn Clean before leaving or within 24 hours.
- Reasonable concerns are reviewed and addressed, though additional charges may apply for added scope.

### 11. Liability
- Pre-existing damage is documented before service when visible.
- Cruizn Clean is not responsible for damage caused by faulty parts, aftermarket installs, or prior vehicle conditions.

### 12. Right to Refuse Service
- Service may be refused or rescheduled for unsafe conditions.
- Service may be refused for biohazards, including mold or bodily fluids.
- Service may be refused for disrespectful behavior.
- Service may be refused when the vehicle was described inaccurately before scheduling.
